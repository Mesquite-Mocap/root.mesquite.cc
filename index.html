Download on the App Store

Features

ARKit SLAM Tracking

Sub-centimeter positioning accuracy using Apple's advanced spatial tracking technology.

Seamless Integration

Connects automatically to Mesquite.cc commercial hardware via local WiFi.

Real-Time Streaming

Continuous position and orientation data for professional motion capture workflows.

Optimized Performance

Battery and display management for extended capture sessions.

Requirements

Hardware Required: Mesquite Root works exclusively with Mesquite.cc commercial motion capture systems. The app cannot be used standalone.

Compatibility: iPhone 6S or later with ARKit support. iOS 14.0 or later. iPhone 12 or newer recommended for optimal performance.

About Mesquite

Mesquite is an affordable, research-grade motion capture system combining body-worn IMU sensors with smartphone-based positioning. Used worldwide by universities, research labs, sports teams, and healthcare providers for biomechanics analysis, animation, rehabilitation monitoring, and human movement research.
