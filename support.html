What is Mesquite Root?

Mesquite Root is developed and maintained by Foxy Ninja Studios in partnership with Mesquite.cc, LLC. The app is a professional positioning sensor that works with Mesquite Mocap dongles, using your phone's advanced sensors to provide precise position and orientation tracking for full-body motion capture on iOS and Android.

System Requirements

🛠 Hardware

Mesquite Root requires a Mesquite Mocap dongle to function. The app cannot be used standalone.

📱 iOS Requirements

iPhone 6S or later with iOS 14.0 or later. iPhone 12 or newer recommended for optimal performance.

🤖 Android Requirements

Android 9.0 or later. Android 12 or newer recommended for optimal performance and sensor accuracy.

Getting Started - Quick Steps

1

Power On Dongle

Power on your Mesquite Mocap dongle and wait for the WiFi network to appear

2

Connect to WiFi

Connect your phone to the Mesquite WiFi network (network name: MM-XX-XX-XX-XX-XX-XX, password: 123456789)

3

Launch App

Launch Mesquite Root app on your iOS or Android phone

4

Mount Phone

Mount your phone on your hip using the provided mounting clip

5

Start Web App

Open Mesquite Mocap web application on your computer or tablet

6

Configure Setup

Configure your motion capture setup in the web application

7

Begin Capture

Begin motion capture - Mesquite Root will automatically stream positioning data to your dongle

Troubleshooting

❌ App shows "DISCONNECTED"

  • Ensure your iPhone is connected to the Mesquite WiFi network (not cellular data)
  • Verify the WiFi network name matches the pattern MM-XX-XX-XX-XX-XX-XX
  • Check that the Mesquite dongle is powered on
  • Try disconnecting and reconnecting to the WiFi network

📊 Poor tracking quality

  • Ensure good lighting conditions for camera-based features
  • Avoid featureless environments (blank walls, uniform floors)
  • Keep phone camera lens and sensors clean
  • Ensure phone has gyroscope and accelerometer sensors enabled
  • Reduce rapid movements during initial calibration phase

🔋 Battery drains quickly

  • Lower screen brightness using the in-app slider
  • Enable Low Power Mode in iPhone settings before starting capture
  • Consider using an external battery pack for extended sessions
