What is Mesquite Root?

Mesquite Root is developed and maintained by Foxy Ninja Studios in partnership with Mesquite.cc, LLC. The app is a positioning sensor that works with Mesquite.cc commercial motion capture systems, using your iPhone's ARKit capabilities to provide precise position and orientation tracking for full-body motion capture.

Requirements

Hardware Required: Mesquite Root requires Mesquite.cc commercial motion capture hardware to function. The app cannot be used standalone.

iPhone Compatibility: iPhone 6S or later with ARKit support. iPhone 12 or newer recommended for optimal performance.

iOS Version: iOS 14.0 or later

How to Use

  1. Power on your Mesquite.cc dongle and wait for the WiFi network to appear
  2. Connect your iPhone to the Mesquite WiFi network (network name: MM-XX-XX-XX-XX-XX-XX, password: 123456789)
  3. Launch Mesquite Root app on your iPhone
  4. Mount your iPhone on your hip using the provided mounting clip
  5. Open mesquite.cc web application on your computer or tablet
  6. Select the commercial suit option in the web application
  7. Begin motion capture - Mesquite Root will automatically stream positioning data

Troubleshooting

App shows "DISCONNECTED"

  • Ensure your iPhone is connected to the Mesquite WiFi network (not cellular data)
  • Verify the WiFi network name matches the pattern MM-XX-XX-XX-XX-XX-XX
  • Check that the Mesquite dongle is powered on
  • Try disconnecting and reconnecting to the WiFi network

Poor tracking quality

  • Ensure good lighting conditions (ARKit requires visible features)
  • Avoid featureless environments (blank walls, uniform floors)
  • Keep iPhone camera lens clean
  • Reduce rapid movements during initial ARKit calibration

Battery drains quickly

  • Lower screen brightness using the in-app slider
  • Enable Low Power Mode in iPhone settings before starting capture
  • Consider using an external battery pack for extended sessions
